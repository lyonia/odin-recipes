Cilantro Pasta

Description

This dish features a creamy sauce prepared with cilantro, cream cheese, goat cheese, and evaporated milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups short pasta, penne pasta, or fusili pasta
  • 1/4 onions
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/3 cup cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup goat cheese
  • 1 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Parmesan cheese, shredded, for garnish

Steps

  1. In a pot, bring to a boil. Add salt and wait until the water comes to a boil again. Add pasta and prepare it following the instructions on the package. Drain and set aside.
  2. For the cilantro sauce: In a blender, combine onion, garlic, cream cheese, goat cheese, evaporated milk, chicken broth, and cilantro and blend until smooth.
  3. In a pot, heat butter and pour in cilantro sauce. Cook for 10 minutes once it comes to a boil. Season cilantro sauce with salt and pepper. Add pasta and mix thoroughly.
  4. Serve creamy cilantro pasta garnished with Parmesan cheese.
