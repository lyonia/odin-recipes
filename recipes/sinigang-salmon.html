Sinigang Salmon

Description

A family-size serving of Mom's delicious sour soup filled with salmon and veggies

Ingredients

  • 2-3 salmon fillets
  • 3/4 bag of spinach
  • 1 radish
  • 1/2 egg plant
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 packet sampalok mix
  • 1/2 packet calamansi mix

Steps

  1. Slice the radish, eggplant, and tomato.
  2. Boil the salmon.
  3. Once it's boiling and you see white stuff separating, discard the water and replace it.
  4. Add in the radish and eggplant. Boil the salmon again.
  5. Once boiling, add in the mixes and the tomato.
  6. Once the tomatoes are cooked, add the spinach.
